pig dice game

Instructions:

  1. Click Roll to roll your die.
  2. Each time you roll your die, that number will be added to your Turn Total.
  3. Click Hold to add your current Turn Total to your overall score and end your turn.
  4. If you roll a 1, your turn total is set to 1 and your turn ends.
  5. The player to reach 100 first, wins!
